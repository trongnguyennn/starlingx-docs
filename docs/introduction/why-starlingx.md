# Why StarlingX

StarlingX is a strong choice when you need a unified, production-ready platform at the edge that supports both virtualized and containerized workloads with operational tooling.

## Reasons to choose StarlingX

- Production-hardened lifecycle management for edge clusters.
- Integrated networking and storage optimized for edge constraints.
- Strong focus on observability, fault recovery, and deterministic behavior.

## Typical use-cases

- Telco edge sites requiring low-latency NFV workloads.
- Industrial automation requiring strict availability and security.
- Distributed clouds where centralized management and local autonomy are needed.
